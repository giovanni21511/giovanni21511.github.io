Screenprint Project

What I did in this project/What I learned:

During this project I made a frame using fourt peices of wood and a peice of fabric to go around the wood. I then crearted my design on adobe photoshop and used a machine to print it out and then iron it onto my frame. After that I then used paint and a squeejy to paint onto a peice of paper, a box, and a colored peice of fabric. The design I went with was me holding the thumbs up with the words "stay positive" warped around my head because in this day in age positivity is something that needs to be spreaded. What I learned during this experiment is, different aspects of adobe while creating my design. I learned how to use different features like puppet warp, changing colors, adding text, the different brushes, the gradient tool, and transform. This prject taught me a lot about adobe and a lot about what I can accomplish as a student and I'm looking foward to learning more.
